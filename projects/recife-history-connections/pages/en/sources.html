Sources · Connections of Recife's History

Reference Material

The data in this project was extracted and systematised from three reference works on the history, culture, and urban geography of Recife and Pernambuco.

Source 01
Trilhas do Recife
Tourist, Historical and Cultural Guide
Author João Batista Meira Braga
Year 2000 (1st ed.) · 2007 (2nd ed.)
Publisher Assembleia Legislativa de Pernambuco
Pages 201

A guide featuring 25 walking trails through Recife, each marked by historical and cultural facts. The work maps streets, squares, churches, and monuments, linking them to the figures and events that made them part of Pernambuco's identity — the primary basis for this project's node and connection structure.

Source 02
O Recife e suas Ruas
Origin, history and nomenclature of Recife's streets
Organization Instituto Arq., Hist. e Geog. Pernambucano
Year 2010
ISBN 978-85-63601-01-8
Pages 420

A reference work covering all neighbourhoods and more than 1,200 streets of Recife — the origins of their names, the historical evolution of each thoroughfare, earlier denominations, and etymology. Essential for understanding how the city's urban fabric reflects layers of occupation, politics, and collective memory.

Source 03
Pernambucanidade
Some historical aspects
Author Nilo Pereira
Year 1983
Publisher Sec. de Turismo, Cultura e Esportes de PE
Volumes 3 volumes

A historical and cultural essay investigating the collective identity of Pernambuco — what makes Pernambuco's culture singular within the Brazilian context. Nilo Pereira explores political, artistic, and social aspects grounded in primary historical documentation, offering an interpretive framework for the roots of Pernambuco's identity.
