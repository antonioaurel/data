About · Conexões da Historia do Recife

About the project

A mapping of the connections between people, places and events in the history of Pernambuco at points that influenced Brazil.

Method

The method combines reading and analysis of sources, identification of the associated places on a map, and building a relationship matrix between the records. This matrix organizes the connections using four main criteria: direct interaction, territorial proximity, historical period, and consequence of the events.

After this stage, the data goes through cleaning, normalization, and quality validation, with continuous corrections whenever new inconsistencies, gaps, or opportunities for improvement are identified.
